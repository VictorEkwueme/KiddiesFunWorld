Are socks required?

Yes, grip socks are mandatory for safety. We provide one free pair with admission, or you can bring your own grip socks.

Can I re-enter on the same day?

Yes! Your admission is valid for the entire day, and you can exit and re-enter as needed. Just keep your wristband on.

Is food included in admission?

Admission covers play area access only. Food and beverages are available for purchase at our cafÃ© at reasonable prices.
