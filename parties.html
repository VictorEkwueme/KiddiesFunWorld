Party FAQs üí°

Common questions about birthday parties

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for weekend parties. Popular dates can fill up quickly!

Can I bring my own cake?

Yes! You're welcome to bring your own cake. Our Deluxe and Premium packages include custom cakes, but you can substitute with your own if preferred.

Are parents required to stay?

At least one parent/guardian must stay for children under 5. For older children, parents are welcome to stay or drop off and return at the end.

What if I have more guests than my package includes?

Additional guests are welcome at $15 per child. We can accommodate up to 50 children total with advance notice.

Can I customize my party package?

Absolutely! We're happy to customize any package to fit your needs. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 14+ days before the party. 50% refund for 7-13 days notice. No refund for cancellations within 7 days.
