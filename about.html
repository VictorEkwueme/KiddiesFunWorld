Our Story üìñ

Kiddies Fun World was born from a simple dream: to create the ultimate play paradise where children can laugh, learn, and grow in a safe and inspiring environment.

Founded in 2015 by parents who understood the need for quality, safe, and engaging play spaces, we've grown from a small play center to a beloved destination for families across the region.

Today, we're proud to have served over 10,000 happy children and their families, hosting countless birthday parties, creating lasting friendships, and fostering a love for active play and creativity.
