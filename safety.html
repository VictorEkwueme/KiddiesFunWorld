Our Safety Commitment

At Kiddies Fun World, safety isn't just a priority‚Äîit's the foundation of everything we do. We've implemented comprehensive safety measures, rigorous training programs, and strict protocols to ensure every child can play, explore, and have fun in a secure environment.

Hygiene & Cleanliness Policy üßº

Maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness

Daily Deep Cleaning

All play equipment, surfaces, and facilities are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized every day before opening and after closing.

Hourly Sanitization

High-touch areas including door handles, tables, and bathrooms are sanitized every hour throughout the day.

EPA-Approved Products

We use only child-safe, EPA-approved cleaning and disinfection products that are effective yet gentle.

Air Purification

HEPA air filtration systems run continuously to maintain excellent air quality throughout the facility.

Hand Hygiene Stations

Multiple hand washing stations and sanitizer dispensers are conveniently located throughout the play center.

Health Screening

Temperature checks and health screening for all visitors and staff to prevent illness spread.

Supervision & Monitoring üëÄ

Ensuring constant care and attention

Trained Staff Supervision

Our certified staff members are positioned throughout the play areas at all times, actively monitoring children's activities and ensuring safe play.

CCTV Coverage

Comprehensive CCTV camera coverage in all play areas and public spaces for continuous monitoring and security.

Staff-to-Child Ratio

We maintain a 1:10 staff-to-child ratio during peak hours to ensure adequate supervision and quick response to any needs.

Parent Supervision Required

Children under 5 years must be accompanied by an adult at all times. Parents remain responsible for their children's supervision.

Controlled Entry/Exit

Single monitored entrance/exit point with staff verification to prevent unauthorized access and ensure children's security.

Wristband System

All children and accompanying adults receive matching wristbands upon entry to verify guardian relationships.

Emergency Procedures üö®

Prepared for any situation

First Aid Response

All staff members are certified in pediatric first aid and CPR. First aid kits are strategically located throughout the facility with immediate access.

Fire Safety

State-of-the-art fire detection and suppression systems, clearly marked emergency exits, monthly fire drills, and evacuation plans posted throughout.

Emergency Contacts

We collect emergency contact information for all children. Staff can immediately reach parents/guardians if any incident occurs.

Medical Partnership

Direct partnership with nearby hospital for rapid medical response. Ambulance can arrive within 5 minutes if needed.

Facility Rules & Guidelines üìã

Please follow these rules for everyone's safety and enjoyment

Required

  • Grip socks must be worn at all times
  • Wash hands upon entry and regularly
  • Supervise your children at all times
  • Be respectful to other children and families
  • Follow staff instructions and safety guidelines
  • Keep wristbands on throughout visit
  • Remove shoes before entering play areas

Not Allowed

  • No outside food or drinks (except baby food/formula)
  • No running in non-play areas
  • No rough play, pushing, or fighting
  • No smoking anywhere on premises
  • No pets (except service animals)
  • No entry if child is sick or feverish
  • No strollers in play areas
